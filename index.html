About Us

Started only recently, at the beginning of 2023, The Himalayan Shire is a wholesome mountain experience. It is a three storey villa, with three rooms on the ground floor, four rooms on the 1st floor and the entire 2nd floor dedicated to recreational and chilling space. We pride ourselves not just with amazingly spacious rooms and washrooms that are unlike any other property’s but more so with our loving and caring hospitality that will make your stay a truly delightful one. We aim and hope to send you back with lots of memories to cherish with an eagerness to visit us again soon.

The Setting

A serene, peaceful countryside location, surrounded by apple orchards on all sides, with an exceptionally beautiful view of the Himalayan range. You will hear nothing but the chirpings of the birds all day and can easily spot some rare and beautiful bird species hanging around our gardens and balcony. The sunrise view from our property is to die for. Sit outside in our lawn and enjoy some deliciously cooked meals prepared by our chef. Have the time of your life in our recreation space with pool and TT table and plenty of board games, enjoy a karaoke session with your friends or just find a quiet corner to relax. We also organise guided hiking tours for the more adventurous among you.

The Location

We are very aptly located away from the noise and traffic of the city but still in the close vicinity of the main highway, markets, tourist spots and hospitals. 19kms away from the main Shimla city, and very close to the famous tourist location Kufri, our location is perfect for both, the lover of off-beat locations as well those who want to do the more touristy stuff.
